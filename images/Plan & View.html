What is a Gantt chart

Gantt charts are interactive bar charts that help managers and teams completely outline and schedule projects on a timeline. They allow you to illustrate and visualize every step from day one to the end, connect tasks with dependent relationships, and track project progress to make sure you’ll meet your projected deadline.

Features

Timeline broken up into days, months, years.
Task dependency lines or arrows
Milestones
Bars to show the full duration of each task
A line to denote the current date within the project schedule
Critical path timeline
